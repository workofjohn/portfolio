Cards

Gouache on Bristol.
9x12.
Completed in the summer of 2016.

Pieces were made in a series of cards for the Design Foundations course at the RISD Pre- College program.

The original card sketches were done in marker, and the gouache was detailed with a black pen to add detail that brushes weren't able to.

The card concepts were based off of mobster ladies and the 1920's- 1950's concept of "gangsters" which led to the darker undertones in the pieces..
