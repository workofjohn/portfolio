Malachite Abstraction

Heavy body acrylic and oil pastel on canvas.
18x24 Inches.
Completed in November of 2016.

Created as a painting from observation with the intent to not paint a standard still life.
Close up of a piece of Malachite.

Heavy body paints were used to enhance the line-heavy pattern of the Malachite.
Oil Pastel was added after in order to enhance some of the colors.

The piece was mostly done by keeping the paint wet to ensure the brush strokes contributed to the pattern.

"MALACHITE"

Abstraction of a Malachite

The Malachite piece was done in order to fulfill a project requirement for a still life, but I had wanted to try something new and thus tried an abstraction.
The heavy body acrylics were used along with trying to keep the paint wet to create the fun swirls that existed in the mineral.

I'm not very happy with the final results, and I don't think that what I tried was very successful.
